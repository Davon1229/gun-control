Introduction

The Social Issue of gun use is a highly controversial topic. Not only does the information displayed in this webpage pertain to the use of guns in the United States but as well as countries other than the United States. That way, one can compare United States gun use to other countries. What will be displayed, is the number of homicides in each country, the number of firearms owned in that particular country, and age restrictions, etc. The point of this webpage is to bring awareness to those who are unaware of how great of an issue the use of guns is.
